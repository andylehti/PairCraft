# Privacy Policy for PairCraft

**Last updated:** March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PairCraft collects, uses, stores, shares, and deletes information when you use the App.

## 1. Overview

PairCraft is a Devvit game jointly owned by **Andrew Lehti** and **Metopedia**, an independent research institute. We aim to minimize data collection and retain only the information needed to provide gameplay, scorekeeping, leaderboard functionality, safety controls, diagnostics, and limited administrative functions.

## 2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect or process:

### a. Reddit and Devvit identifiers

* Reddit user ID
* Reddit username
* subreddit name
* post ID
* comment ID
* run ID and similar internal identifiers

### b. Public profile-related data

* public display name or username
* public avatar URL, if available through Reddit platform access

### c. Gameplay and app data

* game mode
* score
* words found
* hints used
* time remaining
* rounds cleared
* streak and averages
* leaderboard entries
* daily challenge participation
* run/session state
* subreddit-specific configuration needed for gameplay

### d. Technical and operational data

* limited cache records
* rate-limit and abuse-prevention data
* audit or diagnostic event records
* scheduler and background-task control records
* preview and metadata caches

We do **not** ask for your Reddit password. We do **not** collect direct messages or non-public Reddit content unless a platform feature explicitly provides it and it is necessary for the Service. We do **not** intentionally collect sensitive personal data such as political beliefs, religion, health information, or similar special-category personal data.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

* run gameplay and validate runs
* maintain leaderboards and lifetime stats
* operate daily challenges and post-specific game state
* prevent abuse, spam, cheating, and technical misuse
* rate-limit requests and protect service availability
* diagnose bugs and maintain app integrity
* comply with Reddit, Devvit, legal, and safety requirements

## 4. Legal/Platform Basis for Processing

We process data as needed to:

* provide the Service you choose to use
* operate and secure the App
* comply with Reddit platform rules and legal obligations
* pursue legitimate interests in maintaining a functional, safe, and abuse-resistant game environment

## 5. What We Store

The App may store data in Redis or related runtime storage, including:

* per-user stats summaries
* daily participation flags
* leaderboard records
* cached public avatar URLs
* active game runs
* per-post and per-subreddit metadata
* temporary preview caches
* configuration records
* share/post linkage metadata
* rate-limit and lock records
* short-lived audit or diagnostic records

Some of these records are temporary and expire automatically. Some are retained longer where needed for stats, integrity, or operational continuity.

## 6. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this Policy. Retention may vary by record type.

Examples:

* active run/session data may be short-lived
* caches may expire automatically after a set period
* leaderboard, stats, and metadata records may persist longer where needed for app functionality
* some user-linked records may be deleted automatically by TTL-based cleanup or scheduled maintenance

We do not guarantee permanent retention of any game results, previews, share data, or history.

## 7. Changes to Rules, Settings, and Stored Data

If PairCraft rules, scoring rules, challenge rules, or subreddit settings change, the App may purge and rebuild substantial portions of stored PairCraft data associated with the affected subreddit.

This may include deletion of leaderboard data, statistics, daily-post records, cached preview data, post metadata, salts, score-thread references, daily indexes, counters, and stored user statistics connected to that subreddit.

Some temporary, short-lived, or separately scoped technical records may not be removed immediately and may instead expire automatically, be overwritten, or be deleted during later cleanup. Users should not expect continuity of gameplay records across major rule or settings changes.

## 8. Deletion and Platform Events

Where Reddit or Devvit deletion events apply, we aim to remove related stored content and retain only minimal metadata where necessary for system integrity, diagnostics, fraud prevention, or platform compliance.

If you contact us with a deletion or privacy request, we will review it in light of the data actually controlled by the App, Reddit platform constraints, and applicable law.

## 9. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

We may share or expose limited data only:

* with Reddit and Devvit platform services as necessary to operate the App
* with infrastructure or service providers that help us operate the App, if any
* when required by law, legal process, or platform enforcement
* to investigate fraud, abuse, cheating, security incidents, or policy violations
* in aggregated or de-identified form where reasonably appropriate

If the App uses approved external services, those services may receive only the minimum data needed for the specific function.

## 10. No Profiling or Re-Identification

We do not use the App to profile users, infer sensitive traits, re-identify users across unrelated datasets, or conduct surveillance-style monitoring.

## 11. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect data, including limiting collection, using controlled storage, restricting secrets handling, and applying abuse-prevention and operational safeguards.

No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 12. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that such information has been collected, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

## 13. International Users

If you use the App from outside the United States, your information may be processed in jurisdictions where data protection laws differ from those in your location.

## 14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will revise the “Last updated” date above and may provide notice where appropriate. Continued use of the App after changes take effect means you accept the revised Policy.
